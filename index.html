Calicos are awesome

A calico cat is a domestic cat of any breed with a tri-color coat. The calico cat is most commonly thought of as being typically 25% to 75% white with large orange and black patches (or sometimes cream and grey patches, which is called a muted calico); however, the calico cat can have three other colors in its pattern. They are almost exclusively female except under rare genetic conditions.
